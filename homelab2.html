CONSTRUCTION BEGINS

Here I've taken some 2x4s and cut them down to stretch across the legs of my tool bench. There was just enough wood leftover from other projects to cut two rails.

They've been spaced far enough apart to hold all of my equipment and even some personal storage.

I've also taken some scrap cuttings and placed them on the front rail to keep hardware from being accidentally pulled off the rails.
